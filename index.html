    Working Process

    01. Brief & Analytics

    First of all, I conduct an interview and learn all the details of a project and the specific details of a business. I research competitors, collect references, form an idea for a project, and develop a structure for a future site.

    02. Prototype & Design

    I design a website prototype while keeping the user experience (UX) in mind. After approval, I develop two visual design concepts (UI) for two blocks (the main screen and the subsequent block). Then I started full-fledged development based on the selected concept.

    03. Layout & Launch

    I begin site construction once the design is approved. I develop applications, connect forms and services, and perform basic SEO setup. Then I test the website for errors and launch it.
